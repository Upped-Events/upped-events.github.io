Why Upped?




Budget-friendly, software-based cashless payments for tickets and products

Sell tickets online or through the app, with unlimited customizations and settings
Sell to attendees not just during the event, but before and after as well

Comprehensive vendor, staff, sponsor, and charity tools

Manage event stakeholder onboarding, set-up, and payment integration in minutes
Source new vendors or staff to your event, keeping track of every contract and invoice
Connect sponsors and charities to your event, earning on ad exposure and supporting noble causes

Real-time alerts, reminders, and security features

Use messages and alerts to keep attendees, vendors, and staff aware of event updates
Prioritize security by linking stakeholders to the app and specifying security settings or alerts for each group

Granular analytics and AI-analyzed event reports

Upped supports data transparency, so enjoy a product that makes every data point available for measurement
As event owners use Upped, our AI identifies trends and recommends actions that can be taken to react to those trends (either in real-time or as action items for next year)

Next-gen attendee engagement

Use an event’s real-time data to create “triggers” that send out messages or discounts to the right customers, at the right time, either manually or through AI automation
Give event stakeholders access to features that are only possible with software, bringing in new revenue and dramatically improving the event experience

Resolution Center

Streamline how you view and respond to feedback and disputes from stakeholders at your event. Prioritize types of feedback and disputes, assign team members to resolve issues, and eventually use Upped’s AI to automate resolutions for you.
