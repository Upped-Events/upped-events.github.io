About Us

Our Story

Back in 2016, our founder Mark and his team were working on a startup to process secure software-only payments at bars. After attending numerous events and speaking with event owners about their pain points, the team realized that existing event management solutions suffered from a serious case of "old and boring". The attendee experience -- arguably the most important aspect of every event -- was being casually neglected because of a lack of technological innovation.

After throwing a few 8,000-person events and crunching the resulting data, it became apparent that our software was both cheaper and more adaptable than any other market solution. After investing time and money into a software-only alternative for every logistical aspect of an event -- Upped Events was born.

Today, Upped Events offers a software-only event management and mobile payment solution that significantly improves the attendee experience and event owner revenues.

Upped Events -- your event improvement partner.



Mission Statement

Upped’s mission is to offer a software-only event management solution that uses AI integration and digital payments to cater to attendee needs while enabling event owners to tap previously-inaccessible revenue streams.

Our Vision

Upped aims to be the world’s best SaaS company for maximizing attendee happiness at events while contributing to event owners’ success.

Our Values

Client focus, innovation, agility, value generation, consistent measurement, transparency, and authenticity.
