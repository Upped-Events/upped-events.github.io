About Us

Maximizing attendee happiness at events while contributing to event owners' success.

Up Your Event Today

Upped was made to improve the event experience. Whether you manage an event or attend it, our platform provides software solutions to event problems that previously required time, money, or hardware to solve. Technologists with an eye for statistics, our team is standing by to create the digital solutions you need to get the most out of your event.




UPayments

No more lines, cash, or physical tokens. Attendees value instant access to amenities and information with Upped’s app right in their pocket.

Order Management

Ticketing

Sell tickets through both our mobile app and UppedEvents.com. Ticket price changes, inventory management, and other advanced features are available in larger plans.

Vendor Management

Streamline customer orders, payments, and reporting for vendors. Geomarketing, profit splits, and real time order tracking are just the start.

Vendor Sourcing

Marketing & Engagements

Meaningfully increase attendee spend by pairing email, SMS, or push notification marketing campaigns with discounts, promotions and coupon codes. Define your target groups manually or have Upped’s AI analyze event data and recommend impactful campaigns.

Marketing Campaigns
