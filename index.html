Up Your Event Today

Upped was made to improve the event experience. Whether you manage an event or attend it, our platform provides software solutions to event problems that previously required time, money, or hardware to solve. Technologists with an eye for statistics, our team is standing by to create the digital solutions you need to get the most out of your event.

Event Owner Benefits

Create and sell tickets through our mobile app and UppedEvents.com
Offer attendees secure cashless payments for every vendor at the event, with customer service functions just a click away
Gain access to never-before-seen revenue streams that are only possible with Upped’s software
Send campaigns to your attendees with custom discounts, coupons, and promotions -- all under the guidance of Upped’s AI
Event owners today want an intuitive, adaptable, profit-increasing, expense-reducing, positive -vibe-inducing event management solution. With Upped, every aspect of your event is able to be customized, automated, and measured -- maximizing growth potential and event safety before, during, and after an event.

Attendee Benefits

See events in your area, buy tickets without crazy fees, and receive live updates from your favorite events
Forget about looking for your friends or event resources: find them right in your app
Get exclusive access to deals & discounts that save you money and predict what you might want in the future
Join an event as staff, making money and seeing how Upped operates from the inside (so you can throw your own event!)
As an attendee, you want to maximize your time exploring and experiencing an event -- not worrying about crowds, disorganization, high costs, or lack of information. With Upped, attendees have every answer at their fingertips: tickets, pre/during/post-event purchases, customer service, event information, and much much more -- right in your pocket.
