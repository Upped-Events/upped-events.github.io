FAQ

General

What sized events can Upped manage?

Each of Upped’s three event subscriptions can scale to any event size -- event owners just need to choose which features they need. Whether it’s a conference with a few hundred participants or a music festival with 100,000, our software-only solutions maximizes the attendee’s time while providing event owners with the tools they need to measure and grow their event.

What type of event does Upped’s software work the best with?

By consolidating the software tools that we have created to solve a specific client’s issue, Upped has found that it is able to offer clients numerous previously-inaccessible ways to make money and engage with attendees. The end result is Upped’s “secret sauce”: we grow by creating smart new features that solve our client’s problems. Our developers update the code daily to ensure that Upped works safely for every event type, whether a multi-day music festival, black-tie fundraising event or virtual trade show.

Can Upped’s app be white-labeled or edited to reflect my company’s design & branding?

Yes! Event owners have access to our demo and design features in Upped’s web portal. With the ability to customize every color, picture, word, and button that appears in the app, clients can become comfortable with the experience they are providing to attendees, staff, and vendors well before an event starts.

Pricing + Plans

What is Upped’s pricing plan?

Event owners (EOs) pay a $2-5 per-attendee fee for the number of attendees at their event that they expect to use the Upped mobile app. This fee is determined by quote only, as it is based on the features an EO needs (higher-level subscription → more features → more expensive price per-attendee). Payment (partial or full) is required to gain access to Upped’s more sophisticated features. EOs will be able to apply any unused attendee spots to their future events.

Is it free to start an event?

Starting an event on UppedEvents.com is free, but -- in order to access advanced features -- an event owner must get in contact with Upped for an event quote. Once your first deposit is paid, you are free to explore the features available under your plan and begin Upping your event!

Is there any way I can test the features in my event subscription before paying?

Yes! Once you create an event on UppedEvents.com, you will be given access to a limited number of features within the event management web portal. All advanced features can only be demoed by scheduling an appointment with one of Upped’s lovely staff, or by receiving an event quote from Upped and paying your first deposit.

Can I customize what modules are in my event subscription?

Upped has created three types of subscriptions for its modules, deciding not to charge on a per-module basis because every event -- and especially every event owner -- values their needs differently. By pricing on a quote-only basis, Upped makes sure that event owners get the features they need for a per-attendee price they are comfortable with.

What features are limited in Upped’s “Lite” event subscription?

Upped’s “Lite” subscription contains only fundamental event functionalities like selling tickets (a maximum of three different types), adding vendors (a maximum of three), adding staff manually (no Staff Sourcing) , processing payments with limited customer service features, and accessing Upped Support. Advanced functionalities like menu changes, refunds and charge-throughs, ticket price changes, inventory tracking, and all advanced analytics, engagement, and AI features are only available via a more sophisticated Upped subscription.

What are the main differences between the Plus and Pro plans?

The Pro event subscription contains all of the same functionalities as the Plus plan, but event owners with Upped’s Pro subscription gain access to its ‘pride and joy’: AI-integrated event management. Offering automation, measurement, and real-time engagement capabilities not found anywhere else, Upped Pro clients experience the future of event management.

Account & Billing

Can I change my subscription plan after agreeing on a quotation with Upped sales?

Yup! You can change your subscription plan at any time, even after you’ve got a quotation from us! Add more attendees, upgrade to use our more sophisticated features, or tack on some of our auxiliary features to your plan. Once you’ve signed your contract with Upped, you can get in touch with an Upped representative at any time to make adjustments to your plan.

How can I change my billing information?

Your billing information can be updated at any time in your account drop down, found in the top right hand corner of the portal. You’ll be able to view and update your billing information and preferred payment methods under the ‘Billing & Payment Info’ tab in the ‘Subscriptions and Banking’ section of the account drop down.

How can I add team members to my organization to help me plan my event?

You can add team members to your organization to help plan and run your events through the organization drop down in the top right hand corner of the Upped Events web portal. Once you’ve opened the organization drop down, simply navigate to the ‘My Organization’ page where you will be able to invite team members to join your organization. Type in a team members email to invite them to join the web portal, and you’ll be able to specify what events or parts of the portal they have access to.

What types of payment methods are allowed, and how many, can I add to my account?

Direct bank transfers, physical checks, electronic checks, and electronic payments such as Paypal are allowed. You are not limited in how many bank or electronic payment accounts you are allowed to add to your account, but we suggest you mark one of your payment methods as a default method to be used throughout the portal.

How do I use different payment methods to fulfill different invoices?

You can specify which payment method to use for a particular invoice, or to specify how others should pay you, by navigating to the particular invoice in the ‘Invoices & Payments’ page under ‘Subscriptions and Banking’ in the organization drop-down in the top right corner of the portal. To add, view, or edit the payment method for a particular invoice, simply click on the ellipses to the far right of the desired invoice and click ‘Edit payment details’ to change the payment method for that invoices. You can also access invoices for a particular event by navigating to the event, clicking on the ellipses to the right of the event name on the left sidebar, and clicking ‘invoices & payments’. You can view and edit payment methods the exact same way on the event level by clicking on the ellipses to the right of the invoice you seek to edit.

How can I change my account password?

You can change the password for your account at any time by heading to your account settings which can be found in the top right corner of the portal in the organization drop down. On the ‘Account Settings’ page you can easily change your password.

How can I make my account more secure?

You can make your account more secure by activating two-step authentication to log in. By activating two-step authentication, you will be prompted to enter a security code sent to your phone or email after entering your password, in order to log in and access the portal. You can activate two step authentication in the ‘Account Settings’ which can be found in the organization drop down in the top right corner of the web portal.

I’m locked out of my account. How do I reset my password?

You’ll be able to quickly and safely reset your password by clicking on ‘Forgot my password’ found under the log in fields when trying to log in to the account. Simply enter the email address associated with your account, and we’ll send a link to that email address to reset your password.

Ticketing & Payments

How can attendees purchase tickets or register for my event?

Attendees can find your event on the Upped mobile app, where they will also be able to quickly and painlessly RSVP, register, or purchase tickets to your event, which can be easily scanned to be admitted to the event.

Does Upped allow multiple registrations or tickets to be purchased in a single transaction?

Yes! Attendees can buy multiple tickets in a single transaction, unless the event owner specifies a per person limit on ticket purchases. Tickets can also easily be transferred from one account to another, so if you need to buy for a friend, the process is quick and painless to make your event experience smooth.

What are UPayments?

UPayments are Upped’s proprietary system of cashless transactions. Attendees can make purchases from vendors right through the Upped mobile app. The attendee enters their payment information, and transactions are processed securely with confirmation from staff members at the event.

What are the benefits of UPayments?

For event owners, Upped’s state of the art analytics suite provides you with real time actionable insights into the transactional trends happening at your event. That data is used to create marketing campaigns and attendee engagements to help increase user spend at your event! For attendees, UPayments are a simple alternative to worrying about bringing your cash or credit cards to events. Make those purchases you want quickly and securely just with a tap of the screen right in your hand at any time during the event!
